Welcome to Nathair

This is a nostalgic Snake style game with a twist. The rules are simple: set your difficulty and score as many points as possible in 2 minutes. Score in the top 10 people to get on our leaderboard.

Every time you eat a pellet your snake will grow in length, the more difficult you make it the more points you can earn.

The snake can be controlled using your arrow keys or the W A S D keys and sound effects are off by default.
